All about our Company and what we represent.

Terminal-A is a company founded in Greece . Our first Steps began on February of 2022 , in the peak of Covid-19 pandemic and our goal was the same from the beggining ; make traveling easier , better and faster for every customer .

Meet our Co-founders and learn about them.

Marios Christos Stamatiou & Ioannis Mastrominas are both students that are currently studying Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Patras. At the age of 22 and 24 they took the initiative to begin building Terminal-A from ground zero , due to the lack of services that are currently provided in our country in the field of booking tickets and orginising custom travel plans with absolute ease.
